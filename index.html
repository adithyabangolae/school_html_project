What is plastic dumping?

Pastic dumping is when a significant amount of plastic that is not recycled and is dumped on land and that creates huge piles of non-recycled plastic. This can affect the environment. Plastic dumping can also affect humans by releasing toxic chemicals into the atmosphere and that can affect people's health. Out of 8.3 billion tons of plastic produced,6.3 billion tons of plastic has become plastic waste. Over 2 million tonnes of plastic waste has dumped into oceans globally this year(2021) so far. The plastic that is dumped into the piles will take 10-100 years to degrade so the plastic is going to stay there for a very long time

Facts about plastic

    • In 2017, Kenya banned the use and sale of plastic bags altogether:many other countries are now following this ban
    • Since the 1950s, 8.3 billion tonnes of plastic has been produced worldwide
    • In the North Pacific ocean, there are six times more items of plastic waste than plankton
    • Plastic kills over 1.1 million seabirds and animals every year
    • Worldwide, 2 million plastic bags are still being used every minute
    • From the 1970s to the 1970s, only a small amount of plasyic waste was produced.
